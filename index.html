Welcome to Data Unity!

Data Unity is an open source web tool that lets people engage with structured data and share their discoveries with others. The tool promotes Open Data, where users can query and publish data both from within their organisation and outside.

The key parts of Data Unity are:

  • Publish: Make your data available to everyone
  • Explore: Find insights in your data using the Data Explorer tool
  • Share: Share your insights widely, both inside and outside your organisation
